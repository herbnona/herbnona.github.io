Shadow Work Saturday

PLEASE READ BEFORE YOUR READ THE PROMPT (especially if you are new to shadow work):

  • Please be aware this prompt may bring up uncomfortable feelings for you. Only sit down and journal when you feel ready to do so.
  • This is a purely private and anonymous journaling exercise, meant only for you. Your response is never saved, stored, nor shared. There is no information about you collected in any way.
  • If this prompt does bring up something you want to discuss, please share it with a friend or use the #trauma-dumping channel in discord.
  • Please note that this is a self-guided exercise and you can choose to do it or skip it of your own volition. If you find you need additional support with a topic raised by these prompts, please seek out close friends, family, or a professional to help you dive deeper.

What is shadow work?

  • a journaling practice meant to help you confront and work with parts of yourself you deem "ugly"
  • a healing practice among people actively working to improve their self-esteem, confidence, or otherwise heal themselves

How can shadow work be helpful?

  • It can help you acheive integration with difficult or unwelcome emotions and memories.
  • It can help you connect to your strengths and intuition.
  • It can help you find grace and compassion for yourself and others.
